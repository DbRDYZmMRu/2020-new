         Where do I start? It has been all hell. I would be denying the much-awaited awakening I deserve if I refuse to admit the loss from the days spent away from my journal. This rain had a reaction out of a lure, and still as I type this, I must run away to see her pictures, like a fiend.

 

         Today is a day of reckoning, I am back to my ways—reading—and it is obvious how far I have strayed, as my reading has become slow and my attention has lost its focus, I’m transfixed by this.

 

         I have deleted her pictures, videos, I’m not happy, I even lost all need for listening to my daily poems. It happened on a morning I set out for the text for the daily poem, I couldn’t find the text in any of the search results so I went for a video, the account read Mr. Butler and that was it, I got engulfed by the laziness it incited. In time I found the enemy, the game-mind saying quietly to go against the grain, when the poem says true, it quietly whispers false, now I must say bye. Now I know, truly I’m all alone, and this is the way it will remain, I certainly feel washed away as I wrote as a comment to a post of Jessica.
