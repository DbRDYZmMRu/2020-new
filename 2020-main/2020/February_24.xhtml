I’ve been thinking about my travel, the commissioner of police my dad told me about, got his appointment, happy about that, also I’m yet to receive the call of invitation for the visit to the native priest, well I hope it never comes, it’s a good morning, the electricity’s been out since yesterday night, it was not a good night, I had damn mosquitos, begging me to quit from my sleep; sucks like spittle on your mane.

I’ll listen in now on the poem of the day, the poem from days ago; the little mute boy, now that’s something.
