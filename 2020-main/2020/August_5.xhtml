         I am confounded, I don’t know why, most of my relationships in the past have been online ones; remote and it’s been fine all this time but this has me confused, lonely and distraught, my thoughts feel so public.

 

          The character of man is consciousness; and the character of consciousness is to consume, perpetually, . . . the man of mind must finally reduce himself knowingly to an endless refusal to be anything whatever . From a book called Monsieur Teste, it’s a great interesting book by the author Paul Valery, and I’ll be reading more from the author.

 

 Consciousness cannot entirely consume being and still continue to exist. It depends on being. Sensibility is its home, knowledge is its profession.
