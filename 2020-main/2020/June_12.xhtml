         I discovered today, that, out of the abusive manipulation and bombardment from the collective—the game-mind—a creeping wave of chorea has set in. I don’t know how certain my prognosis is, but my body’s reaction; my face threw jerky movements to the right of my upper jaw, just below my cheeks. I was trying to switch my attention; my attempt was a very brusque one. This reminds me of the day my dad came around when I was having breakfast, and he got upset over the fact that I had an egg with my meal, further dismissing, of the unimportance of my Left Ventricular Hypertrophy, my pains, and imposing further his will, taking away his pillowcase. I tried to make light of the mood, a flinch from what? . . . resulting in anxiety.
