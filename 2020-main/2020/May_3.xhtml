Devotional: SHOW, NOT TELL, WHAT YOU KNOW

 

Poem of the Day: Tartary by Walter de la Mare

 

 

 

 

Entries:

 

         I still can’t reason my way out of not loving life and my budding self, in time I’ll blossom.

 

         For in much wisdom, there is also much sorrow (Ecclesiastes) an allusion to the state of the West and its constant dissolution of previous knowledge, the quest of the endless seeker who unsettles all things.
