Author's Note

 

         I started this program of devotional rituals . . . keeping records of my daily activities like major lessons learned in a journal (a later inclusion), and writing. This was on the 1st of January of 2020. I lose my laptop in a robbery incident, leaving me with no device to continue my resolve. In the early hours of Sunday, January 19, 2020, armed robbers burgle my home, leaving with my laptop, phone, among other valuable items removed from the property. I was in disarray, a result of this perturbation, it takes two weeks, finally I align my resolve to my resolute and continue with my minds work.

         In later considerations on how best to improve my journal, Alighting at the improviso of including poem text when available. In doing this I peel off the web strands of old poems, discovering how important the days I missed were; both on my devotionals and daily poem readings. The acute exposure of this fact has lead me to include this note. I’ll be doing a revision of the days missed, including in this journal what exist nowhere, not in my previous laptop or in some cloud somewhere ;)

         Out of love for poems, I’ll be including my favourite poems from days recorded on my previous laptop.
