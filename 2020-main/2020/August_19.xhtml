         Isn’t this what life is? A tad bit off normalcy, and all together weird. Well, I was listening to the Anthropocene podcast by John Green, the episode been  Indianapolis and Love at first sight , and I must say I learned some of what the real—in quote—American life is like, Indianapolis being a model state or instance of this. What’s driven for this post is the pellucid serendipity—actually the two listeners who sent a mail, requesting to have these subjects reviewed; that is: Indianapolis, and Love at first sight. Listening to the first topic in reviewal, I could not but try to ebb back my thoughts from cascading in a domino of plausible events in the life of I and my love. Him talking about his lawn, and I thinking of my dread for laborious activities, but not willing to pass the buck if it means losing my girl. Yes, I know it’s a bit tripe, but enough of that. What’s mind boggling here is the fact that Indianapolis was suggested by Jessie, and love at first sight was suggested by Theo, huh? Jessie and Theo, really that’s some oddity, of all episodes, of all names? Okay, I guess I’m growing, I’m hearing voices, and now the world is some beautiful odyssey where I get my girl and we live forever after if I make the right choices. Great, I guess God isn’t so tyrant after all. What do you think? lol.
