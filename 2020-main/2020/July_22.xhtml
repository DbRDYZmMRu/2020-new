         I wrote a prose for Jessica yesterday, after causing an uproar, posting more than I usually do, I ended up deleting all my post, now the first post on her Instagram is her in a brown gown. Here is the post I wrote for her.

 

 Jess every shade of you is my bower, 

 A synergy of pat showery moods, 

 Soaking me up in misty bliss 

 Evanescent, sensational. 

 You’re my cuddle turf, 

 My fraught lust 

 A posit point.
