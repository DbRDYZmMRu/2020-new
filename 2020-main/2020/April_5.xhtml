Devotional: TRUST, BUT VERIFY

 

Poem of the Day: First Word (After Helen Keller) by Rachel Rooney

 

 

 

 

Entries:

 

         Today’s poem draws a prose (I’m not sure of its metric) around the experience of the first blind and deaf woman to graduate from university - Helen Keller - in 1904. Today, the 5th of April marks the first day; the journey into the land of words, in 1887. With one hand to the flowing water of a pump and the letters for water spelt on the other palm, in those moments capturing the mystery of language as it revealed itself to her.
