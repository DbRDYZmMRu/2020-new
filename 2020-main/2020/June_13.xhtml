         I stopped journaling, I really don’t know why, I guess I suck at it, maybe not, I feel washed out, like I’m less of my authentic self. I guess the least I’ve felt this way is when writing; my songs. Maybe I can experience this also when journaling, I’ll be taking out time for this.
