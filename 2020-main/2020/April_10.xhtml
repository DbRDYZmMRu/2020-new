Devotional: JUDGMENTS CAUSE DISTURBANCE

 

Poem of the Day: Do Not Stand at my Grave and Weep by Mary Elizabeth Frye

 

 

 

 

Entries:

 

         Today is the anniversary of the Good Friday agreement of 1998, a key moment in the Northern Island peace process. 

 

         I lost track of objectivity, the appraisal of self in the driveway of altruism, the servile trait that has got me misplaced in a world of expletive cries of non-committal pandering. Change is in motion, its process, the only viability of my reality.

 

         Music is like porn to my ears, Wearing nothing by Dagny.
