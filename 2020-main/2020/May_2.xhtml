Devotional: BE THE PERSON YOU WANT TO BE

 

Poem of the Day: The Merry Month of May by Thomas Dekker

 

 

 

 

Entries:

 

         Today, a day of love. Listened to what is Love by your one solely loving TheoPhiL, and I lost count already. I miss Esperanza, I think I do more than that, I’m kinda lost but found, a longing for a time that wasn’t perfect but heartfelt.  I am visions of the future  —   a menacing parasitical emblem of my fortitude to pacify the suffering of the present. Creating it is.
