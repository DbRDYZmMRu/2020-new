Devotional: THE STOIC IS A WORK IN PROGRESS

 

Poem of the Day: A Handsome Young Fellow Called Frears by Michael Palin

 

 

 

 

Entries:

 

Dang, listening to Jessica Andrea, I’m melting. Rousing emotions of the first album’s stream. I wish I had all that back, but hey I still get something right? Hate the attempts at cognitive narrowing, an attempt at leaving me a fodder, and constant harassment. My only inference being the not needed biological dad in my life to feel safe, sheltered or any of all that.

 

         Weed was pernicious, I had to stop.

 

         I learned the  Lo-Shu  and created one for myself. It follows a simple rule, all rows, columns, and diagonals add up to 15, the centre numbers must be odd numbers—the yang of the  Lo-Shu —the four corners must be even numbers—the yin of the  Lo-Shu .

 

         I learned the technique of solving the unknown values of a rectangle, using the square method - knowledge of area squares and rectangles - really interesting. Yeah, I was cracking for a while, tinkering with formulae,  figuring it out . To think of how desolate the militants of cognitive deterrence acted, switching the scales between the blacks - the pentatonic - and white - the diatonic, hampering in intention, alluding to the parity of my own task consequence. I will remain on this journey, improving cognition, severing all psychotic tendency.
