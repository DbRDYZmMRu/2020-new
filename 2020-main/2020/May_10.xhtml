Devotional: DON’T BE INSPIRED, BE INSPIRATIONAL

 

Poem of the Day: For my Niece by Kate Tempest

 

 

 

 

Entries:

 

         Careful, lest the items accorded in recognition of being an inspiration, become inspirational to self. Instead relish in the imbibition of the virtues that holding up, still will leave not a less accolade unmerited.
