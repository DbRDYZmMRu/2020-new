 Caged away a bird, peeled. 

 It had perched on my sill. 

 Beak clack! Clack! 

 Clack! Clack! 

 Strange for a call 

 Now out park. 

 Tap! At the sash 

 Clack! At its glam 

 It pares. Pares it seems 

 Din here, din there; on seam 

 In beams on its shimmery plumage 

 I wrest him in my hands cup siege. 

 And now in a mirror cage 

 A singing page he stages 

 Beak clack! clack! 

 Clack! Clack!
