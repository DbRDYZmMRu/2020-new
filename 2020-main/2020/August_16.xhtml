Today's Musical: PARTITA NO. 3 IN E MAJOR, BWV 1006 1: PRELUDE BY J. S. BACH (1685–1750)

 

 

 

 

Entries:

 

         All I have gone through—the troubles of the collective, the game mind—is pushing me to a more nuanced way of living, an existence I greatly deserve.

 

As the day’s devotion says, anything can be pivoted to an advantage, as an albatross in the past as I thought, I’ll go on to kill this animal, and raze its home.

 

Water, water everywhere nor any drop to think.

A sadder and a wiser man.
